FAQ

Common questions answered clearly.
How much notice do you need to plan a trip?

For short regional trips 7 to 10 days is sufficient. For complex multi-country itineraries allow 3 to 8 weeks depending on visa and availability constraints.

Do you handle visas and entry requirements?

We provide guidance and a checklist. We do not provide legal advice. For specialized cases consult the relevant embassy or a certified service.

Is travel insurance included?

Insurance is not included by default. We will supply recommended policies and an explanation of coverage options based on the destination and activities.
